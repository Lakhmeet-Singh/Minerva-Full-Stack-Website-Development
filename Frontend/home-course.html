    COURSES


    The courses we currently offer are English, Maths and Science.

    By enrolling into these courses, students can set the foundation for themselves
    and pave their path to learn more advance topics.

    Maths

    Description: In this course student will be introduced to Maths. Mathematics is essential to many different careers and life in general. By studying for this Maths course you will be learning and applying foundation level of mathematical knowledge and achieve a qualification in a very versatile subject. After this course student can move on to achieve a higher level qualification in mathematics and use their qualification in many different walks of life.


    Course Content: The course will include the following units in their own specific levels:


    • Numbers
    • Calculations
    • Fractions, Percentages and Ratio
    • Mesurement
    • Shapes and Movement
    • Handling data
    • Algebra
    • Geometry and Measure
    • Probability
    • Statistics

    Levels: The course is consist of three levels: Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced. As you progress to different levels you will learn more advanced topics.


    Assessment: For each level of the course, student will be able to test their knowledge by taking a test only one time. They can view thier results anytime and reflect on thier results.


    Support: The most important tool that all of our students have at their disposal is their tutor. When studying for a course with us you are assigned a personal tutor with indepth knowledge and experience in your chosen subject.

    Science

    Description: In this course student will be introduced to Science which consist of Biology, Chemistry and Physics. Biology is the study of living things and, by studying our Science course, students will gain an insight into the natural world. Chemistry is a complex and interesting topic that can form the basis of a career. Students will encounter a range of concepts that further their knowledge of this interesting topic. Physics will teach student about applying scientific reasoning to various situations as well as develop a deep understanding of scientific and technological processes. This course covers a number of units to give a great understanding of concepts and principles that are found throughout the world of physics. This qualification provides the base for a science-related career or for further study at a higher level.
